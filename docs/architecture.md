# Xai Intelligence Workspace Architecture

## Product Narrative
- From raw data to structured intelligence to actionable insight to AI automations.
- The experience is calm, technically confident, and optimized for engineering leaders and decision-makers.

## System Composition
- Hero section: immersive 3D particle morphing canvas with interactive state toggle.
- Insight flow: scroll-triggered cards describing the pipeline.
- Dashboard preview: executive view with animated tab switching and telemetry visuals.

## Design Tokens
- Backgrounds: zinc 950 to black.
- Surface cards: zinc 900 with glassmorphism and blur.
- Borders: zinc 800 at 1px.
- Accent: blue 500 to purple 500.
- Typography: Inter or Geist Sans, with strict spacing cadence of 4/8pt increments.

## Motion Principles
- Framer Motion for declarative UI transitions.
- GSAP ScrollTrigger for sequential reveal timing.
- Three.js / React Three Fiber for hardware-accelerated particle morphing.
